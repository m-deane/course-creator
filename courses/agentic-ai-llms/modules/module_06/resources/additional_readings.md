# Additional Readings

## Papers

- [Red Teaming Language Models to Reduce Harms (2022)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2209.07858) - Anthropic's foundational work on adversarial testing of LLMs
- [Constitutional AI: Harmlessness from AI Feedback (2022)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.08073) - Framework for training models with safety principles
- [Measuring Massive Multitask Language Understanding (MMLU) (2021)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2009.03300) - Benchmark for evaluating model capabilities across domains
- [TruthfulQA: Measuring How Models Mimic Human Falsehoods (2021)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2109.07958) - Benchmark for detecting hallucinations and false statements
- [On the Opportunities and Risks of Foundation Models (2021)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2108.07258) - Comprehensive analysis of safety risks in large models
- [Jailbroken: How Does LLM Safety Training Fail? (2023)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.02483) - Study of adversarial attacks on safety guardrails

## Documentation

- [OWASP Top 10 for LLM Applications](https://owasp.org/www-project-top-10-for-large-language-model-applications/) - Security risks specific to LLM applications
- [Anthropic's Responsible Scaling Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/index/anthropics-responsible-scaling-policy) - Framework for safe deployment of increasingly capable models
- [OpenAI Safety Best Practices](https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/safety-best-practices) - Practical guidelines for building safe applications
- [Google's Secure AI Framework (SAIF)](https://cloud.google.com/security/ai) - Enterprise security framework for AI systems
- [NeMo Guardrails Documentation](https://github.com/NVIDIA/NeMo-Guardrails) - Open-source toolkit for adding programmable guardrails to LLM applications

## Evaluation Frameworks & Tools

- [RAGAS: Retrieval Augmented Generation Assessment](https://docs.ragas.io/) - Framework for evaluating RAG systems
- [LangSmith Evaluation](https://docs.smith.langchain.com/evaluation) - LangChain's platform for testing and evaluating LLM applications
- [Phoenix by Arize AI](https://docs.arize.com/phoenix/) - Open-source observability and evaluation for LLM applications
- [Promptfoo](https://www.promptfoo.dev/) - Tool for testing and red-teaming LLM applications
- [Giskard](https://docs.giskard.ai/) - Testing framework for ML models including LLMs

## Tutorials & Blogs

- [Anthropic's Guide to Red Teaming](https://www.anthropic.com/research/red-teaming) - Systematic approach to adversarial testing
- [Building Effective Guardrails for LLM Applications](https://www.pinecone.io/learn/llm-guardrails/) - Practical implementation patterns
- [LLM Security: Prompt Injection and Beyond](https://simonwillison.net/2023/Apr/14/worst-that-can-happen/) - Real-world attack vectors and defenses
- [Evaluating RAG Systems: A Practical Guide](https://blog.langchain.dev/evaluating-rag-systems/) - Metrics and methods for RAG evaluation
- [Microsoft's AI Red Team Methodology](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/security/blog/2023/08/07/microsoft-ai-red-team-building-future-of-safer-ai/) - Enterprise approach to AI safety testing

## Standards & Guidelines

- [NIST AI Risk Management Framework](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework) - Government framework for AI safety and risk management
- [EU AI Act Compliance Guide](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/) - Regulatory requirements for AI systems in Europe
- [ISO/IEC 42001 - AI Management System](https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html) - International standard for AI governance

## Case Studies

- [How Anthropic Tests Claude for Safety](https://www.anthropic.com/index/core-views-on-ai-safety) - Real-world safety evaluation process
- [OpenAI's GPT-4 System Card](https://cdn.openai.com/papers/gpt-4-system-card.pdf) - Detailed safety analysis and mitigation strategies
- [Production LLM Security Incidents Database](https://github.com/leondz/garak) - Collection of real-world security failures and lessons learned
