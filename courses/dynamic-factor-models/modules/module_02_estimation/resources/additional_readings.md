# Module 02: Additional Readings - Estimation Methods

## Maximum Likelihood

### 1. Durbin & Koopman (2012) - Chapter 7
**"Time Series Analysis by State Space Methods"**
- Complete treatment of ML estimation via state space methods

### 2. Harvey (1989) - Chapter 3
**"Forecasting, Structural Time Series Models and the Kalman Filter"**
- ML for structural time series models

## EM Algorithm

### 3. Shumway & Stoffer (1982)
**"An Approach to Time Series Smoothing and Forecasting Using the EM Algorithm"**
- *Journal of Time Series Analysis*, 3(4): 253-264
- Original EM for state space models

### 4. Watson & Engle (1983)
**"Alternative Algorithms for Estimation of Dynamic Factor Models"**
- *Journal of Econometrics*, 23(3): 385-400

## Bayesian Methods

### 5. Carter & Kohn (1994)
**"On Gibbs Sampling for State Space Models"**
- *Biometrika*, 81(3): 541-553
- Forward-filter backward-sample algorithm

### 6. Kim & Nelson (1999)
**"State-Space Models with Regime Switching"**
- MIT Press - Bayesian approach to state space estimation

### 7. Koop & Korobilis (2010)
**"Bayesian Multivariate Time Series Methods for Empirical Macroeconomics"**
- *Foundations and Trends in Econometrics*, 3(4): 267-358

## Computational Methods

### 8. Commandeur & Koopman (2007)
**"An Introduction to State Space Time Series Analysis"**
- Practical guide with software examples

## Software

- **statsmodels**: Production ML implementation
- **PyMC**: Bayesian MCMC
- **Stan**: HMC sampling for complex models
